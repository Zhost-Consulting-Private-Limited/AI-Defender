# Compliance Alignment Guide

## SOC 2 (Security, Availability)

- Centralized access control with RBAC and MFA.
- Audit logging for admin and policy actions.
- Change management evidence via CI/CD and signed releases.

## ISO 27001

- Risk assessment workflows mapped to Annex A controls.
- Asset inventory of endpoints and telemetry systems.
- Secure development lifecycle and supplier integration controls.

## HIPAA

- Minimum necessary access policy for sensitive telemetry.
- Encryption in transit and at rest.
- Audit trail retention and breach investigation support.

## GDPR

- Data minimization and configurable collection scopes.
- Purpose limitation with tenant-defined monitoring policies.
- Retention controls and right-to-erasure workflows where applicable.

## PCI DSS

- File integrity monitoring on cardholder data environment systems.
- Centralized logging and time synchronization.
- Access control and privileged activity monitoring.

## Evidence Exports

- Policy history
- Incident timeline and actions
- Admin access logs
- Control coverage and compliance gap reports
